Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

🔒 TL;DR: Your immigration data is stored on your device, encrypted with AES-256. We do not sell, share, or access your personal documents. Premium users’ encrypted backup is stored in Supabase and cannot be read by us.

1. Who We Are

StatusVault is an immigration document tracking application available at www.statusvault.org. We are not affiliated with USCIS, the US Department of State, or any government agency.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Enter

When you use StatusVault you may enter document types and expiry dates, personal names for you and family members, document numbers (optional), notes related to your immigration documents, and travel history dates.

All of this data is stored locally on your device using your browser’s localStorage. It is not transmitted to our servers unless you are a Premium user with cloud backup enabled.

2.2 Authentication Data

If you create an account we use Supabase to manage authentication. We collect your email address for login purposes. We support magic link (passwordless) and Google OAuth sign-in.

2.3 Premium Cloud Backup

Premium subscribers may enable encrypted cloud backup. When enabled, your data is encrypted on your device using AES-256 with a key derived from your user ID and email before being uploaded to Supabase. We cannot read your encrypted backup data.

2.4 Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising trackers. No third-party analytics are embedded in the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide document expiry tracking and reminders within the app
  • To authenticate your account via email magic link or Google OAuth
  • To sync your encrypted data across devices (Premium only)
  • To send email notifications about expiring documents (only if you enable this)

4. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored locally in your browser by default. We use AES-256 encryption for any data that leaves your device. Your encryption key is derived from your account credentials and is never stored on our servers.

For cloud backup (Premium), we use Supabase as our backend provider, which is SOC 2 Type II compliant and hosted on AWS infrastructure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not display advertisements. Your immigration data is never shared with government agencies, employers, or any other third parties.

Third-party services we use: Supabase (authentication and Premium backup), Google OAuth (optional sign-in), and Resend (optional email notifications).

6. Your Rights

  • Access — Export all your data as JSON at any time via Settings → Export Data
  • Delete — Delete all local data via Settings → Reset All Data
  • Account deletion — Delete your account and all cloud data via Settings → Delete Account
  • Portability — Import your data on any new device using the JSON export

7. Children’s Privacy

StatusVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us through our app at www.statusvault.org or via statusvault@kkbcori.com.
